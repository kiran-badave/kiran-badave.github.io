## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Issue: Not Showing in Search Results

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 1-2 weeks after submission
2. Check robots.txt isn't blocking
3. Verify sitemap is accessible
4. Check Google Search Console for errors
5. Ensure pages are mobile-friendly
6. Check for duplicate content

### Issue: Low Ranking

**Solutions:**
1. Improve content quality
2. Add more relevant keywords
3. Get backlinks from quality sites
4. Improve page speed
5. Enhance user experience
6. Update content regularly

### Issue: Pages Not Indexed

**Solutions:**
1. Submit URL in Search Console
2. Check for crawl errors
3. Verify robots.txt allows crawling
4. Ensure sitemap is correct
5. Check for noindex tags
6. Improve internal linking
